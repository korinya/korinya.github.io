Korinya En Route Tour

The Korinya En Route Tour brings Korinya's music to New York City, Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Cleveland, Detroit and Toronto in a series of concerts for the first time ever. Playing all-acoustic instruments, Korinya is composed of violin, accordion, traditional drum (бубон), upright bass, and traditional wooden flute (сопілка). Join us for energizing village music, played by the young Korinya: Ukrainian Folk Band.
