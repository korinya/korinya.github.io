Vesnianky by Korinya

Available for streaming on all major platforms:
Spotify iTunes Apple Music Amazon Music

Interweaving several Ukrainian spring calling songs, Korinya’s original arrangement Vesnianky features variations on these centuries-old songs in a dynamic and powerful interpretation.

    CREDITS
  • Alina Kuzma: Vocals + percussion
  • Nick Hladio: Bass + cello
  • Sana Shepko: Vocals + violin
  • Zoya Shepko: Vocals + accordion
  • Ivan Dusanowskyj: Recording, mixing + overdubs
